Show diversity and realism in imagery

Getting older is not equivalent to slowing down, and all older people do not inherently like the same things. Build work without stereotypes

DO

Represent older people as vibrant and with a zest for life (e.g., enjoying sports, entertainment, shopping, dining, working, volunteering, etc.). Avoid focusing heavily on medical settings when portraying older people.
